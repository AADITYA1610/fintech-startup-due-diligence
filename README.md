# AI-Powered Startup Due Diligence Platform

A data-driven platform for evaluating startup viability, growth potential,
financial health and investment risk.

This project is being developed as part of an IBM internship project by
Group DS12.

## Project Overview

Startup due diligence requires investors to evaluate multiple areas of a
business before making an investment decision. This platform organises and
analyses startup data across the following categories:

- Company information
- Founders and team
- Product or service
- Market opportunity
- Financial health
- Customers and growth
- Funding history
- Legal compliance
- Investment risks

The system will calculate financial indicators, generate category-level
scores, identify risk factors, produce visual dashboards and provide
data-driven investment insights.

## Planned Features

- Startup data-entry form
- Financial metric calculations
- Founder and team assessment
- Market opportunity analysis
- Customer growth analysis
- Legal and compliance checklist
- Rule-based risk scoring
- Machine-learning risk prediction
- Interactive Plotly dashboards
- Startup comparison
